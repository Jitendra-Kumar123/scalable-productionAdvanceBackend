Authentication System mai char cheeje hoti hai 
1.validation - validation ka matlab hota hai details/ data ka ek dum sahi hona yani data/details ka format sahi hona hai
matlab data ek dum valid ho kuch bhi galat na ho , jese form mai bahut sare fields hote hai suppose agar mene mob. no.

2.verification - eska matlab data sahi hai ya nahi , man lete hai ek email hai wo check karne ke liye ki wo 
ek real user ya data sahi hai to hum use verify karne ke liye OTP bhejenge

validation - data ka format sahi hona 
verification - data jo hame mil rha hai sahi hona ya check karna / server par jo data aa rha hai wo sahi hai ya nhi

4.authorization - ek user kya kya kar sakta hai matlab set of permissions
